**Multi-Layer Shadow System:**
```

3. Distant shadow for depth
4. Far shadow for drama (on hover)
```

**Results:**

- Buttons have tactile quality
- Dropdowns have clear hierarchy
- Depth without heaviness

**Sidebar:**
- 20px backdrop blur with 180% saturation

- Feels modern and sophisticated

**Page Header:**
- Sticky positioning with backdrop blur
- Frosted glass effect
- Content readable through slight transparency
- Professional and refined

### 4. **Purposeful Animations**

**Page Load:**
- Stat cards slide up with staggered delays
- Creates sense of progression
- Feels intentional and polished
- Smooth cubic-bezier easing

**Hover States:**
- Cards lift slightly (1px) with enhanced shadow
- Buttons scale down on click (0.98)
- Icons scale up on hover (1.1)
- All with 200ms smooth transitions

**Dropdowns:**
- Scale-in animation from 96% to 100%
- Feels natural and intentional
- No jarring pop-ins
- Smooth and professional
